# Amazon Product Reviews - Data Visualization Report

## Objective

The objective of this project is to transform Amazon product review data into meaningful visualizations that provide business insights.

---

## Dataset

Amazon Product Reviews Dataset

---

## Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## Visualizations

- Customer Rating Distribution
- Recommendation Distribution
- Top Brands
- Top Categories
- Average Rating by Brand
- Review Length Distribution
- Rating vs Recommendation
- Rating vs Review Length
- Most Active Reviewers
- Correlation Heatmap
- Dashboard

---

## Key Findings

- Customer satisfaction is generally high.
- Amazon products receive the highest number of reviews.
- Most customers recommend the products.
- Negative reviews tend to be more detailed.
- Visualization makes customer behavior easier to understand.

---

## Conclusion

The project demonstrates how effective data visualization transforms raw data into meaningful insights that support business decision-making.
